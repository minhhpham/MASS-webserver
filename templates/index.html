WELCOME!


This tool is intended to help civil engineers and policymakers make sustainable decisions in the design of integrated wastewater management systems. The tool will ask for information about the population clusters that will be under consideration and potential water-treatment locations, then use the provided information to mathematically minimize the costs and environmental impacts and to maximize the value of water recovery. Finally, the recommended designs (including treatment trains and the scale of implementation) are presented through intelligible visualizations.

To start using the tool, please go to Projects page and create a new project.

To view a quick demonstration, please take a tour of the Demo.

For guidance in using this tool, please read the Documentation page.

If you have questions or feedback, you may find the best method to contact us via the Contact page.
